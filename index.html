New York MacArthur Leadership Institute Introduction

The Macarthur Leadership Institute is a New York-based professional education consultancy. Based on abundant local resources in the United States, we provide global companies with professional leadership training courses, organize benchmarking business exchanges and cross-national high-level conferences in the foreword areas of science, technology and finance. This institude is designed to promote cross-border exchanges and rapid development of enterprises, to create excellent organizational skills, and to build learning organization.

The MacArthur Leadership Institute program in New York City focuses on seven major management functions and has designed a comprehensive curriculum of integrated capacity building that includes seven major components: organization, operations, strategy, marketing and sales, corporate finance, business technology, and integrated management skills. The 33 capacity-building modules in the business function area apply to different audiences, including chief executive, middle management and change backbone respectively.

The MacArthur Leadership Institute in New York focuses on topical or frontier topics such as leadership, innovation and entrepreneurship, future science and technology, financial science and technology, and organizes benchmarking business visits and exchanges and the US Frontier Summit. Helping global entrepreneurs connect with the most cutting-edge theories and practices in current East Coast America and open the "door of knowledge" to global entrepreneurs for the future.

So far, MacArthur Leadership Institute in New York has served more than 100 enterprises and more than 800 entrepreneurs from China, South Korea, Britain, Africa and other countries.
